Our Vision

The Lord has called us to fulfill a very definite purpose, which is to take His divine presence to the peoples and nations of the world, and to demonstrate the character of His Spirit everywhere. When you worship with us, you learn more than just the letters of the Word; you’re imparted with and impacted by the Spirit of the Word. As we share God’s Word, it takes root in you, and you become exactly what the Lord wants you to be. The Holy Spirit gets a hold of your life, and His vision becomes real to you and in your life.
